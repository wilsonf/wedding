Our Story

Our journey starts with Coincidence but it ends with Love

  • First Met

    September 2, 2012

    Wilson was a senior to Clairine in The University. Although we attended different major, we joined the same organization. However, we never had the chance to talk with each other until one day during an organization's field trip, Wilson offered Clairine a ride a back home. Thus, it's our Love at "first ride".

  • First Date

    September 21, 2012

    Clairine just finished a stressful exam. Then Wilson used that as an excuse to treat her lunch. Thus was also how Clairine trapped into Wilson's upcoming dates

  • In A Relationship

    November 2, 2012

    After a few months of being close to each other, Wilson brought Clairine to a romantic dinner and confessed his feelings out of nowhere. Clairine accepted his confession happily. Then our chapter of life begins.

  • LDR

    2014 - 2016

    Wilson pursued his Master in Taiwan for 2 years, while Clairine continued her study in Indonesia. Despite long distance relationship, we still loved and cared about each other. There was no single day we lost contact of each other. Our trust grows.

  • Engaged

    July 9, 2018

    Early in the morning, we were walking by the beach when Wilson suddenly got on his knee and pulled out a ring saying, "Will you Marry Me?". Clairine's heart stopped for the longest time ever, then she said "Yes!". After being in a relationship for 6 years, we were both excited to open a new chapter of our life.

  • Wedding Day

    February 9, 2020

    We are sincerely thankful to our family and friends, for being a part of our journey somehow. Thus we would like to invite you (Yes, you!) to together opening our new life chapter. It would be a great honor to have you in our special day. :)

Wilson & Clairine
